Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

Thank you for playing Sky-Fall: Endless Platform Adventure. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

Sky-Fall does not collect or store personal information directly. However, our game may utilize third-party services that collect limited data to provide functionality.

2. Third-Party Services

Our game uses the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand game usage patterns. These tools collect anonymous data such as:
    • Feature usage statistics
    • Game performance metrics

For more details on how Google services process data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. App Permissions

Sky-Fall only requests permissions necessary for core gameplay functionality. These may include:

  • Storage access (for saving game data locally on your device)

4. Data Storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. You can delete this data by uninstalling the application.

5. Children's Privacy

Sky-Fall is designed for general audiences but is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our game, please contact us to have it removed.

6. Your Choices

You can control certain data collection through your device settings:

  • Reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Disable analytics collection through your device settings (if available)

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: skyfall.support@gamedev.com.
